NASA Artemis II Moon Mission: NASA's Artemis II mission is set to be the first crewed mission of the Artemis program, aiming to send astronauts around the Moon and pave the way for future lunar exploration. This mission will test critical systems and demonstrate the capabilities needed for sustainable lunar presence.


Agency : NASA


Launch Date : 2024


Mission Objective : Crewed lunar flyby


Significance : Artemis II will be a crucial step in NASA's plan to return humans to the Moon and establish a sustainable presence, enabling future missions to Mars and beyond.


Duration : Approximately 10 days

SpaceX Starship Test Flights: SpaceX continues to conduct test flights of its Starship spacecraft, designed for interplanetary travel. These tests aim to validate the vehicle's performance, safety, and reusability, bringing humanity closer to Mars colonization and deep space exploration.
